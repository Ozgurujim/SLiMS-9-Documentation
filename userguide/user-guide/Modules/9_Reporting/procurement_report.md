#### Procurement report

------

Displays a collection procurement report, giving a table of Year received and  Classifications broken into the Main Classes . There is a further subdivision of procurements into titles vs items in each Class, and totals are provided for  years and Classes. 

Options buttons provide for obtaining procurement lists for each year, which can be exported in spreadsheet format; and for a procurement bar chart.
